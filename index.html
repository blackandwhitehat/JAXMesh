📡 Coverage gaps? We hear you. Here's the plan.

After migrating to MediumFast, we've seen some coverage gaps. As a group, we've decided to hold off on switching back to LongFast while straggler nodes are still cutting over. There are plenty that still need to flip.

We're giving until April 24th to improve MediumFast penetration. After that, we'll run a community poll to decide whether to stay on MediumFast or switch back to LongFast.

If we can get the coverage dialed in, MediumFast sets us up well for the future. If not, we'll use this time to collect the data we need to understand what gaps to fill before trying again.

The good news? We're fortunate to be learning these lessons now, while the mesh isn't critical infrastructure. A year ago we had basically no coverage. Today we have a real network with real gaps to solve. That's progress.

Thanks for your patience. Want to weigh in? Join #project-medfast on Discord.
